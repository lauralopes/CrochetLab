# 🎧 Headphone Covers Crochet

**Category:** Accessory  
**Technique:** Circular crochet  
**Level:** Intermediate

---

## 🧶 Materials

- **Hook:** Prym Ergonomics 4.5 mm  
- **Yarn:** Velvet Círculo  
  - Length: 165 m  
  - Weight: 100 g  
  - Tex: 606  
  - Composition: 100% polyester  
  - Color: 940  
  - Lot: 1023

---

## 🪡 Pattern

1. Magic ring with 6 single crochet (6)  
2. 6 increases (12)  
3. *1 sc, 1 inc* repeat (18)  
4. *2 sc, 1 inc* repeat (24)  
5. *3 sc, 1 inc* repeat (30)  
6. *4 sc, 1 inc* repeat (36)  
7. 36 sc (36)  
8. 36 sc (36)  
9. 36 sc (36)  
10. *4 sc, 1 dec* repeat (30)  
11. *3 sc, 1 dec* repeat (24)  
12. 24 sc (24)  
13. Sew to close the circle
